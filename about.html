About Simply Found

Why this site exists and how recommendations are chosen.

Our Purpose

Simply Found was created to make finding good products easier. The internet is noisy, overwhelming, and often driven by hype. This site focuses on clarity, usefulness, and thoughtful curation.

How Products Are Selected

Products featured on Simply Found are chosen based on quality, design, value for money, and real-world usefulness. Recommendations are kept minimal and honest — no exaggeration, no unnecessary pressure.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on this site are affiliate links. This means Simply Found may earn a small commission if you choose to purchase through them, at no extra cost to you. These commissions help support the site and keep it free to use.
